Blurbs

About me:

Records may be dead in the digital age, but here you'll find my news on new and vintage album art.

Who I'd like to meet:

Music and art lovers.

Interests

  • General

    My parents had an extensive record collection I used to admire as a child, listening to the record and staring at the album art to feel more connected to the music. Writing, music, art, technology, and participating in new experiences are my primary passions.
